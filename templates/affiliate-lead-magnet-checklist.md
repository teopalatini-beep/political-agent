# 5-Step Starter Checklist

## Goal
Launch your first profitable affiliate content system in 14 days.

## Step 1: Pick one pain point
- Choose one recurring pain point for your audience.
- Define one concrete outcome your content promises.

## Step 2: Build one simple offer stack
- One digital offer (primary commission)
- One Amazon backup offer (lower friction purchase)

## Step 3: Publish 5 faceless videos this week
- 3 software/AI angle
- 2 fitness angle
- Use one CTA format in all five for clean measurement

## Step 4: Capture leads from day one
- Add one clear lead magnet CTA in every caption.
- Keep your opt-in page focused on one promise.

## Step 5: Review metrics on day 7
- Watch retention at 3 seconds
- Link click-through rate
- Email opt-in rate
- Initial conversions
