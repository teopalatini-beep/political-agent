## Fast production workflow (per video)

1. Record/capture screens (10-15 min)
2. Voiceover with TTS (5 min)
3. Edit in CapCut with subtitles (15-20 min)
4. Export 9:16 (1080x1920)
5. Publish with disclosure and CTA

Target per video: 30-40 min once template is ready.
